Ready.. GO!

crw is a web-app created by 6 Computer Science students at the University of Utrecht.

We have completed a useful and working application for rowing teams to log and monitor health and performance over time. Coaches can compare individual rowers easily by using graphs generated using ChartJS.

Make the most out of your crw.

crw is responsive.
This means you can enter your health data and log your training anywhere, anytime, on any device with a browser.
This means you can log your resting heart rate while still in bed, and analyze your performance with your coach, just before participating in a race.

Get your own crw*

We kindly invite you to try crw. Here's how it works:

  • Register (and make your teammates do so too!)
  • Log in and create a team
  • Add rowers and coaches
  • Track your health and performance!

Easy peasy, huh? It is supposed to be, so you can train more and worry less about complicated stuff.

* Oh, and it's free, too.
