Ready.. GO!

crw is a web-app created by 6 Computer Science students at the University of Utrecht.

Our goal is to complete a working and useful application for rowing teams within a timespan of 8 weeks.

Make the most out of your crw.

With crw, rowing teams can plan their training schedule and communicate their ergometer or boat performances efficiently with their coaches. Coaches can track their crew's total and each rower's individual performance and fitness.

No more spreadsheets with erg scores.
